Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, email address, phone number, and farm location to personalise your experience. Crop scan images are processed locally and results stored in your private Firestore database. Weather data is fetched using your device GPS coordinates and never stored with personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide AI-powered farming insights, weather intelligence, and personalised recommendations. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore & Firebase Auth) with strict per-user security rules. Only you can read or write your own data. Firebase complies with SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR standards.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Google Firebase (auth & database), Open-Meteo (weather data — no personal data sent), IP-API (approximate region detection — anonymous), Nominatim/OpenStreetMap (reverse geocoding — anonymous).

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@smartfarm.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Contact

For any privacy concerns, email us at support@smartfarm.app
